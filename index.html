About

I grew up in the care system in County Durham. Two days after graduating from Northumbria University with a degree in International Relations and Politics, I joined HM Treasury's newly established Darlington Economic Campus through the Civil Service Care Leaver Internship Scheme.

Today I work as a Business Relationship Manager in Treasury Business Solutions, sitting between the department's people and its technology. My focus is making digital change land well: aligning stakeholders, strengthening strategic partnerships, and helping colleagues build confidence with new tools, including AI.

Outside the day job, I co-founded and co-chair the cross-government Care Leavers' Network, supporting care-experienced staff across departments and widening the routes into public service. That lived experience shapes how I think about technology too. Systems, whether social or digital, work best when they are designed for the people with the least power in them.

I write about business relationship management and public sector digital practice, and I completed TechUP's AI Foundations programme, where my capstone was an Ethical AI in the Public Sector workshop for practitioners navigating government AI adoption.
